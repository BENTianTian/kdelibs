How to get the multimedia chapter in the KDE2.0 Book

There is a KDE2.0 Development book which is freely available (under OpenContent license) includes a good introduction about how to develop with aRts. It doesn't cover all aspects, but should give a good overview.

It's Chapter 14. You will find the book online at http://www.andamooka.org/kde20devel. If you want to go directly to the download of the chapter and/or sourcecode in various formats, you might want to look here.
