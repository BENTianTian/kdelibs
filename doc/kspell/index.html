The KSpell Handbook

David Sweet, <dsweet@chaos.umd.edu>

Last updated 5 Oct 1999

KSpell is the spelling checker used by KDE applications such as KEdit, KMail, KRN, and KWord. It is a GUI frontend to International ISpell and ASpell. (If you do not have Ispell installed you can obtain it from the International ISpell home page. ASpell is available from the ASpell home page.)
