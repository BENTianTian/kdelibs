The KSpell Handbook

David Sweet, mailto:dsweet@chaos.umd.edu
Last updated 6 May 1999

KSpell is the spelling checker used in KMail, KEdit, KWrite, and KRN. It is a GUI frontend to International ISpell. (If you do not have Ispell installed you can obtain it in RPM format from the Red Hat FTP site, or in source form from the International ISpell home page.)
